Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, most of our lessons and exercises are completely free. We also offer premium courses with advanced features, but you can start learning without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can access many resources without an account. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress, save your lessons, and receive personalized recommendations.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing with our word lists, flashcards, and interactive quizzes. We also encourage reading short articles and using new words in writing or conversation exercises.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we provide digital certificates for certain structured courses. Once you finish all lessons and pass the final quiz, you’ll receive a certificate that you can share or download.
